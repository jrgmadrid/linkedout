Judge settings

The LLM judge is optional and BYOK. Your key is stored in this browser only (chrome.storage.local, never synced) and is used solely by the extension's background worker.

Leave empty and save to clear. Without a key here or in config.local.js, the heuristic tier runs alone. Any OpenAI-compatible endpoint. A non-default host also needs adding to host_permissions in manifest.json, or Chrome blocks the call.
